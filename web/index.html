EMF Camp Real-Time Telemetry Portal

Welcome to the Open EMF Camper Portal! This system tracks the real-time campsite telemetry, location coordinates, step counts, and active statistics of our participants during the Electromagnetic Field (EMF) Camp.

Each camper carries a sensor-rigged tracking device that logs activities. Our live portal displays the following telemetry metrics:

  • 🍺 Drinks Consumed (beers logged in the field, coffees, water)
  • 💩 Toilet Visits (pee and poo frequency charts)
  • 🚶 Physical Activity (cumulative walking steps and estimated distance)
  • 📈 Environmental data (ambient noise amplitude and local temperature)
  • 💳 Monzo Budgets (live campfire expenditures and transactions)
